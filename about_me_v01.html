Who is

Tomer Rozenberg?

Hi, I'm Tomer, a UX/UI Designer with a rich background in education. I have been designing user-centered training programs for new teachers and refining English teaching curriculums to meet user needs, boost satisfaction, and enhance revenue. My passion is connecting with people through user research and designing solutions that resonate with users. With experience in management positions, I excel at aligning design strategies with business goals. I bring a unique blend of interpersonal communication and analytical thinking to UX/UI design, crafting intuitive user journeys that align with users' mental models.

What Interests Me?

My favorite part of my job is user research. I love how showing interest in people, makes them feel important and willing to share. That's how I learn what I should design.
I love aesthetics, and I love creative processes that take time to come to fruition. My next big thing to learn and improve is Brand Identity Design. I find it so exciting! That's why I'm taking an online course on everything Adobe these days. To create brands that owners are proud of and people are attracted to.

Oh, and when I'm not busy doing design things. I like learning from other designers, lectures, or newspapers. I do photography, I love dancing and doing a lot of sports, as much as I have the time for.
